furniture structural load simulation

3D modeling

A medium sized aquarium may weigh up to 200kg. Not all furniture can handle this weight. Therefore, it may be a good idea to make sure it can handle it beforehand. Shown here are some furniture designs which use aluminium profile frames. Load simulations were performed on these. The structures turned out to be too weak. These sims saved me the trouble of buying expensive aluminium frames, only to find they buckle under the weight.
