Hey!

I'll talk a little about myself here. I'm a bit of a nerd, with interests in technology, science, and engineering. Learning, improving myself and my work are my biggest motivators.

My strongest strengths are:

  • Deep focus
  • Eagerness to learn
  • Patience
  • Perseverence
  • Attention to detail
Some weaknesses:
  • Learning how to plan better
  • Social settings can be a challenge

At the moment I'm studying mostly programming. Other topics that I'm studying and are electronic engineering, 3D modeling and printing. Some examples of these can be found in the Works section.

At around age 19 I started studies in engineering at Hogeschool Utrecht. These were never finished, however, due to circumstances that were unknown even to me. After a some years I got diagnosed with Asperger's Syndrome, which explains why my studies failed. Social and busy settings. Funnily enough the interests I developed independently fit the engineering studies I never finished. Full circle.
